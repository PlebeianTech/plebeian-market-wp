== Description ==

Plebeian Market is a distributed peer-to-peer, value-4-value alternative to Ebay and Amazon for sovereign individuals and merchants who are transitioning to the bitcoin standard.

It's a marketplace where anyone can sell anything (legal). All you need is a Bitcoin wallet and you'll start selling or auctioning your stuff for Bitcoins in no time and in a sovereign way.

You will own your market stall and you'll be happy!

== Features ==
* Ability to manage your Plebeian Market products from WordPress
* Configurable widgets that can be added at posts or pages
* Your customers can buy your products directly from your WordPress blog
* (Soon) Ability to create auctions
